# About Us

BlockEden.xyz is a San Francisco-based software company that helps web3 developers connect to blockchain nodes and networks instantly and securely. We aim to build an all-inclusive platform for web3 developers in the next five years, starting with a genesis product that could immediately serve the Aptos and Sui developer community's needs.

Our world-class team is made up of blockchain and infrastructure engineers who have previously worked at Google, Meta, Uber, and IoTeX. We're committed to making it easy for developers to build applications on the blockchain and are excited about the potential for web3 to change the world for the better.
